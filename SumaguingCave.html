“Sumaguing Cave, located in southern Sagada, is a thrilling adventure spot for both experienced and first-time spelunkers. Managed by the local tourism office for safety, the cave offers stunning stalactites, stalagmites, and unique rock formations shaped over centuries. Having visited twice, I’ll share my travel guide and experience exploring one of Sagada’s mystical caves.”
